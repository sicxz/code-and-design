How it works:
localStorage.setItem('key', 'value') — saves data
localStorage.getItem('key') — retrieves data
localStorage.removeItem('key') — deletes one item
localStorage.clear() — deletes everything
DevTools tip: Open DevTools → Application tab → Local Storage to see your saved data!
