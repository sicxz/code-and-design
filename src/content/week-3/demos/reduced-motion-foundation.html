Decorative Motion (Should Be Reduced)

These animations are purely decorative. They should be disabled or minimized when reduced motion is preferred.

Hover Card

Hover over me. The lift effect is decorative — it adds delight but isn't essential.

Continuous Animations

Bouncing, pulsing, sliding — all decorative.

Functional Motion (Consider Keeping)

Some motion serves a purpose: loading indicators, progress feedback, focus states.

Loading Spinner

Loading data...

A spinner tells users something is happening. Without it, they might think the page is broken.

Button Focus State

Focus rings (the outline when you Tab to an element) should always be visible. They're essential for keyboard navigation.
