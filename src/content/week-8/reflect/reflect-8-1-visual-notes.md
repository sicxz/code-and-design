# Reflect: Visual Notes — Scroll Timeline Map

**Points:** 10 (Complete/Incomplete)
**Time Estimate:** 15–20 minutes

---

## TL;DR
Map your scroll timeline: which sections trigger where, what animates, and what the user sees at each scroll position.

---

## Submit
1. Scroll map uploaded to Canvas

---

## The Prompt

Draw a vertical scroll timeline for your site. For each section, show:

1. **Trigger point** — Where in the scroll does the animation start?
2. **Animation type** — Trigger-and-play or scrub?
3. **What animates** — Which elements? What motion?
4. **What the user sees** — What's in the viewport at this scroll position?

Think of it as a scroll storyboard — a bird's-eye view of the entire scroll experience.

**Format:** Vertical timeline with sections marked along it. Annotate each section with trigger type, animation, and pacing notes.

---

## Evaluation

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|

| Trigger points and animation types labeled | 3 |
