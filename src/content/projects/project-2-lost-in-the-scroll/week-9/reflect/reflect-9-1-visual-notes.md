Final reflection on the project. What did you learn about storytelling through code?

---

## The Prompt

This is the last Reflect of the project. Answer these questions — in writing, as a sketch, or as an annotated screenshot:

1. **The Metaphor:** Did your metaphor hold up? Did it change from what you planned in Week 5? How?

2. **The Hardest Part:** What was the most frustrating or challenging part of this project? How did you get past it?

3. **The Best Part:** What's your favorite section or moment in your site? Why?

4. **The Code:** Pick one code pattern you're proud of understanding (a timeline, a ScrollTrigger config, the reduced-motion check). Explain what it does in plain language.

5. **The Growth:** Look at your Week 0 visual notes. Compare what you knew then to what you know now. What's the biggest change?

6. **If I Had More Time:** What would you add, change, or refine?

---

## Why This Matters

Week 10 presentations ask you to reflect out loud. This written reflection is your prep. The students who do this well present with confidence.
