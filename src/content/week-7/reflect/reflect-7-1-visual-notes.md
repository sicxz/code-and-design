# Reflect: Visual Notes — Rough Cut Audit

**Points:** 10 (Complete/Incomplete)
**Time Estimate:** 15–20 minutes

---

## TL;DR
Annotate screenshots of your rough cut to show what translated well from Figma, what still needs work, and how readable/structured the browser version is.

---

## Submit
1. Annotated rough cut screenshots uploaded to Canvas (sketch, digital, or screenshots with notes)

---

## The Prompt

Take screenshots of your live site (desktop view; mobile optional but encouraged) and annotate them.

Include at least:

1. **Section structure check** — Mark all major sections (hero, chapters, closing). Is the story order clear?
2. **Figma translation check** — For 2 sections, note what matches your Blueprint and what changed in the browser (and why).
3. **Visual rhythm check** — Mark where the page feels dense vs spacious. Does the pacing feel intentional?
4. **Content + hierarchy check** — Mark one place where content hierarchy is clear and one place that still feels confusing.
5. **Accessibility baseline check** — Add notes showing:
   - heading order / section labels are clear
   - one contrast pair you verified
   - one place you'll refine focus/motion behavior in the upcoming Polish Pass

**Think of this like a design QA pass** — you are auditing the rough cut before the polish and JS animation layers start.

---

## Evaluation

| Criteria | Points |
|----------|--------|
| Screenshots clearly show major sections / structure | 3 |
| Figma-to-browser translation notes on at least 2 sections | 2 |
| Visual rhythm / pacing annotations present | 2 |
| Content/hierarchy annotations present | 2 |
| Accessibility baseline notes included (headings/contrast + next-step note) | 1 |
