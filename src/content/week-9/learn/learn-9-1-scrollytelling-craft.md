# Learn: Scrollytelling Craft

**Points:** — (not graded, but come to class ready to discuss)
**Time Estimate:** 15 minutes

---

## TL;DR
Watch a pinning tutorial. Look at one advanced scrollytelling example and notice the pinned sections.

---

## What to Do

### 1. ScrollTrigger Pinning (Required — 8 min)
[GreenSock: ScrollTrigger Pin](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bFxPJfDCPnM) (start at pinning section)

Watch for:
- How `pin: true` locks a section in the viewport
- How `end: "+=100%"` controls the scroll range
- How content animates while the section is pinned

### 2. Study One Pinned Section (Required — 7 min)

Open [Apple AirPods Pro](https://www.apple.com/airpods-pro/) and scroll slowly through the product page.

Find at least 2 sections where the viewport "locks" and content animates within the pinned area. Ask:
- How long does the pin last (how much scrolling)?
- What animates during the pin? (text, images, 3D model?)
- When does the pin release? Does it feel natural?

**Come to class ready to share what you noticed.**
