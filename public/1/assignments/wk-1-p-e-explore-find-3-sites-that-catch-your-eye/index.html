Instructions

Set up your column

Title it with your name, then drop in 3 websites that do something well.

Look for things like:

  • Typography or color doing real work
  • Layout that guides your eye intentionally
  • Navigation that feels different from the default
  • Animation or interaction that serves the content (not just decoration)

For each site

Add a short note (1–2 sentences): what specifically caught your attention, and why you think it works.
