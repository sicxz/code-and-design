Instructions

Step 1: Complete Your Work

Create three separate CodePens for the following exercises, use CodePen 2.0, use your own images, and create your own content. I don't want a copy of what's on codedex.io; apply what you learn there and create the following. 

  1. Newspaper – Create a newspaper article layout
  2. Lost Pet – Design a lost pet flyer
  3. Favorite Band – Build a page about your favorite band
  4. Complete the Challenges (they can be found here in Milanote. 

Step 2: Submit Your Links

Copy and paste the URLs for all three CodePens in the text entry box below.

Format your submission like this:

Newspaper: [your CodePen URL]
Lost Pet: [your CodePen URL]
Favorite Band: [your CodePen URL] 
Challenges: [your CodePen URL ]

 

Example Submission:
